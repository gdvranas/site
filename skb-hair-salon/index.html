Our Story

A salon built on trust, craft & community

From the day we opened our doors in Niles in 2000, we've earned our spot as a top salon. We offer unparalleled beauty pampering while respecting our clients' busy schedules. Our mission is to make every client look stunning and feel their best.

Founded by Sandy Brillakis — originally from the beautiful island of Crete, Greece — SKB is a proud Greek-owned small business woven into the fabric of the Niles community.
