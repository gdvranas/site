What We Do

Our Services

From small dings to full collision restoration — every job is treated with the same hands-on attention to detail. No shortcuts. No surprises.

Collision Repair

Full structural and cosmetic restoration after any collision. We work to manufacturer specifications, restoring frame integrity, panels, and finish to pre-accident condition.

Dent Removal

Precision paintless dent repair (PDR) and traditional techniques for door dings, hail damage, parking lot impacts, and more — preserving your original paint wherever possible.

Paint & Refinishing

Factory-matched color mixing, multi-stage application, and a flawless clear coat finish. Your car will leave our shop looking exactly as it did the day it rolled off the line.

Rust Repair

Cut-out and replacement of rust-compromised panels, metal fabrication, and protective coating application. We stop rust in its tracks before it spreads further.

Insurance Claims

We work directly with all major carriers. Our team handles the adjuster communication, documentation, and approvals so you don't have to deal with the paperwork.

Free Estimates

No obligation, no pressure. Stop by or call and we'll give you a clear, honest assessment of the work needed and an accurate quote before anything is touched.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do you work with insurance claims?

Yes. We work directly with all major carriers — State Farm, Allstate, Geico, Farmers, Progressive, and more. We handle the adjuster communication, documentation, and approvals on your behalf so you can focus on your life.

How long do repairs take?

It depends on the extent of damage. Minor dents and paint work can often be done in 1–3 days. Major collision repairs typically take 5–10 business days. We provide a precise timeline with every estimate so you're never left guessing.

Do you offer free estimates?

Absolutely — every estimate is completely free and no-obligation. Call us at (847) 657-8181 or stop by our Glenview shop during business hours. We'll give you a clear, honest assessment right away.

Is Lekas a family-owned shop?

Yes, 100%. Mark Lekas founded the business in 1989 and remains personally involved in every repair. You'll often speak directly with Mark — no corporate layers, just a craftsman who stands behind his work.
