Our Story

More Than a Meal — A Downers Grove Legacy

Eagle Restaurant was founded in 1981 by Lee Zografos, a Greek immigrant with a passion for hearty food and warm hospitality. On the site of a former gas station on Maple Ave, Lee built something far more lasting — a community gathering place.

Today, his son John Zografos carries on that tradition, serving the same recipes, the same smiles, and the same dedication to quality that have made Eagle a Downers Grove landmark for over four decades.

From Greek-inspired gyros to Chicago-style hot dogs and hand-pressed burgers, every plate reflects a family's love of good food and their community.
