Our Story

A Family's Love
for Greece, Delivered Daily

"Everything here came from home — and home is worth sharing."

Founded by John Brillakis, a native of Chania on the island of Crete, Brillakis Import Foods has been a cornerstone of the Chicago Greek community for decades. Today, his son Manolis carries on that mission — scouring Greece for the finest imports and bringing them directly to the shelves of their Niles storefront.

Walk in and you'll find barrel-aged feta wheels fresh from Greek cooperatives, cold-pressed olive oils from century-old Cretan groves, rare spirits like tsipouro and raki that you simply cannot find anywhere else in Illinois, and a warmth of service that makes every visit feel like a homecoming.
